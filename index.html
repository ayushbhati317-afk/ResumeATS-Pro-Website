      FAQ

      Frequently Asked Questions

      What is an ATS and why does it matter? +
      ATS (Applicant Tracking System) is software used by 99% of Fortune 500 companies and most SMEs to automatically filter resumes before a human ever reads them. If your resume doesn't match the keywords and format requirements, it gets rejected automatically.
      Is my resume data stored or shared? +
      No. Your resume text is sent to the AI for analysis and the result is returned to you. We do not store, share, or use your resume content for any other purpose.
      How accurate is the ATS score? +
      Our scoring model is trained on ATS best practices from tools like Workday, Greenhouse, Taleo, and Lever. While no tool can replicate every ATS perfectly, our analysis covers the most critical factors: keyword density, section headers, formatting, and quantified achievements.
      What score should I aim for? +
      A score of 75+ is considered good for most positions. For competitive roles or large companies, aim for 85+. If you're below 60, focus on the Critical suggestions first.
      Can I check multiple versions of my resume? +
      Yes! Free users can check up to 3 times per day. Pro users get unlimited checks — perfect for tailoring your resume to each job application.
